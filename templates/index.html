XAI Fact-Checking System

Verify geopolitical claims with explainable AI

Enter a claim about USA–China relations. The system classifies it as True or False using a fine-tuned BERT model, then explains why using SHAP token attributions.

Try an example

China originated COVID-19 in a Wuhan laboratory
The US imposed 25% tariffs on $200 billion of Chinese goods
The Chinese balloon shot down over the US was a weather research vessel
Huawei equipment poses a verified national security risk to the United States
China claims Taiwan is an inalienable part of its territory
